Create a low-fidelity wireframe for the 'Search Results' page of a hotel booking website. The platform is desktop web.

- **Primary Goal:** Help users scan and compare hotel options easily.
- **Key Information/Elements to include:**
- Sort & Filter options
- Interactive Map view
- List of hotel cards (with image, price, rating, key amenity)
- Primary CTA: 'View Deal' button on each card
- **User Context:** The user has just entered their destination and dates and now wants to find the best value.
- **Constraint:** The filter options must always be visible on the left side of the screen.

Annotate the wireframe in terms of information hierarchy and element placement.
